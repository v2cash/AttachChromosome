AttachMemory

C++ Command line Based Injector By 2cash

Using AttachMemory Is Simple, as its command based, all you need to know are the commands.

How to use

If you wanted to inject a dll into an application, you could do the following:

attachmemory.exe -process hl2 -inject thiscooldll.dll

If you wanted to inject multiple dll's into an application, you could do the following:

attachmemory.exe -process hl2 -inject thiscooldll.dll -inject thiscooldll1.dll

If you wanted to inject dll's into multiple applications, you could do the following:

attachmemory.exe -process hl2 -inject thiscooldll.dll -process mspaint -inject badpaint.dll

If you wanted to inject multiple dll's into multiple applications, you could to the following:

attachmemory.exe -process hl2 -inject thiscooldll.dll -inject thiscooldll1.dll -process mspaint -inject badpaint.dll -inject yungleanmode.dll

You can also now delay injections, here is an example:

attachmemory.exe -process pbuster -inject disablepunkbuster.dll -wait 2500 -process bf2 -inject thishack.dll
